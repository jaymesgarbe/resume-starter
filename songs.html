Do you like this song?



Fav Part of Song

The chorale-like voices in the very beginning, followed by Kendrick's monotonous introduction, has me electrified every time I listen to this song, and every time I start this album.

Overall Review

This song is a beautiful, offbeat return of the esteemed Kendrick Lamar. He takes us down a windy, unpaved road full of cultural trauma with which he is learning to heal. Through an amalgamation of beat, flow, and tempo changes, it feels like the song itself needs further assembly. It's a masterful thematic arrangement for the album that follows. It's impressive on its own, but please enjoy it along with the rest of the project.
