Song of the Week

Week 3: Lisa Sawyer - Leon Bridges

Overall Rating: 9.3/10

Listen Here

Spotify

Youtube

Fav Part of Song

Literally any word this man speaks is heaven-sent. That being said, I absolutely adore how he sings the line "the complex-ion of a sweet praline". The following rhymes of that stanza flow so beautifully and arrhythmically imperfect as well.

Overall Review

I was fully prepared to put a different song in place (a certain late-summer bombshell by Nicki Minaj), yet I heard this song for the first time in quite some time and realized that this just needed to take the spot. Applause.
