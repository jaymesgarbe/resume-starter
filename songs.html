Overall Review

This week, there's a new Taylor Swift album. I was undoubtedly obliged to put one of the tracks as my Song of the Week, so here we are. Is Karma my favorite song off the album? Overall, probably not. After listening to the album on repeat three times over on release night, however, this little diddy has been trapped in my mind. It would really be unfair to pick any other song. I hope you enjoy it!
