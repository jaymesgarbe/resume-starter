Week 9: Songs for Women - Frank Ocean

Overall Rating: 9.4/10

Do you like this song?



Fav Part of Song

I indeed have a favorite verse/stanza from this song and it is as follows (second chorus): "And every time a n**** ask if I sing songs to get at women, I say nah, he said: 'who do you think you're kidding, I know you, you stole my girlfriend.'" This line concludes the little narrative that Frank tells throughout the song, and is a pretty enjoyable conclusion at that.

Overall Review

*DISCLAIMER* Please don't get too attached to this song like I have, it is not on ANY mainstream music streaming services.

The production of this song alone makes this track worth plentiful praise, but what sets it apart from all the other amazing tracks on this mixtape is the attractive story-telling within. I'm not going to outline the story here, you can perceive it for yourself, but take notes of it while appreciating the equally impressive production. If you need more of this mixtape, I will put a link to my favorite place to listen to it (it is very good), but mind the disclaimer, it truly is a slippery slope.

Overall Review

Although this artist might not fit the usual taste of mine, his vocals and lyricism have had me hooked for weeks. This song describes Rod Wave’s experience in gang life and how he has used music to escape. His lyrics and flow carry the heavy subject matter beautifully. I hope you’ll enjoy this song as much as I have :)
