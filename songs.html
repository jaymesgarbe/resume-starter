Fav Part of Song

Both of Pharrell's short verses break up the overly repetitive chorus in a way that makes this song as enjoyable as it is. I also thoroughly enjoy the anomalous nature of Andre 3000's finishing verse.

Overall Review

The production of N.E.R.D's album from which this track originates is powerful and upbeat. This song's chorus gets repetitive (I listen to the song on repeat for the full duration of writing my review), but the verses on this track are really exciting and it's a lovely song to just throw on when you are going about your day.

Celebrity Song of the Week

Zoe Campion: Pancakes for Dinner - Lizzy McAlpine

Overall Rating: 8.5/10

Listen Here

Spotify

Youtube

Overall Review

I listened to this song flying into Oakland and cried, because for some reason tears are my natural impulse on landing planes. Lizzy McAlpine’s lyrics speak to insecurities so poignantly, sometimes I feel like she’s reading my mind. The gentle instrumentals really accentuate her lyricism, and the crescendo during the bridge matches the emotional swell. Despite being a bit fatalistic, the sentiment is cute. Very much a woodsy vibe, so listening takes me back to being in northern Minnesota.
