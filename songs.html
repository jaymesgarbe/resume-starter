Song of the Week

Week 7: BALD! - JPEGMAFIA

Overall Rating: 8.7/10

Listen Here

Spotify

Youtube

Do you like this song?



Fav Part of Song

JPEGMAFIA, who I will from now on be now referring to as Peggy, says "BALD" three times within this song. While they are all extremely pleasing to the ear, the last "BALD" is so reverberant and real to me. It is my favorite part of the song and indeed the last lyric of this track.

Overall Review

While not lyrically intricate or astute (this is plainly a song about his unfortunate hairline), BALD! is still very exciting. Peggy is a master of accreting his beats into a conglomeration of samples, percussion, and random sounds; He makes it work every time, this song included.

Mackenzie: You the One Fish in the Sea - Liv.e

Overall Rating: 9.5/10

Listen Here

Spotify

Youtube

Overall Review

What an awesome song! I’ve been very much enjoying it for a while now and it’s become one of my favorites. It reminds me of older jazz rap in the sense that the music is somewhat repetitive, but in a way where the artist manages to put a really unique and interesting spin on it. Liv.e’s voice is so pretty and she’s such a cool person in general (take a look at her Instagram). I would highly recommend the rest of the album as well:)
