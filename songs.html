Song of the Week

Week 15: Endlessly - Omar Apollo

Overall Rating: 9.5/10

Listen Here

Rate this song!

/10



Fav Part of Song

I once again find myself having an extremely hard time choosing my favorite part of this song. The vocals throughout the song are very pleasing, but I do believe one set stands above the rest. The pitched-up vocals in the chorus do something really nice for me and although that's a rather bland pick for my favorite part of this song, I will adhere to my brain's inclination.

Overall Review

This is my favorite song from Omar Apollo's most recent album "Ivory (Marfil)". This week, I had the pleasure of seeing him live, and this song sounded SO good coming right from his vocal chords. I had to put it in the spotlight! If you want a semi-live experience of this song, check out his NPR Tiny Desk on youtube, I highly recommend.
