Song of the Week

Week 2: Slide Around - Chance the Rapper

Overall Rating: 8.3/10

Fav Part of Song

There are so many good parts of this song inlcuding when chance goes "ch-ch-chia" and Nicki's adlibbing throughout the song. "No Grammy, still a goat, though" from Queen Nic has to take the top spot though.

Overall Review

A hidden gem in an otherwise underwhelming debut album by Chance the Rapper. Without the wealthy contribution of Nicki Minaj, this track would likely blend in with the rest.
