Fav Part of Song

The incredible vocalization of these high school boys astounds me. The sweet and simple harmonics create such a gleeful energy that carries throughout.

Overall Review

This song was popularized by the two extremely popular samplings "Bound 2" and "A BOY IS A GUN*" by Kanye West and Tyler, the Creator respectively. It's an uncomplicated love song by this group of highschool boys (two sets of twins "plus one" interestingly). Indulge in this song if you need a bit of simplicity in your life.

Celebrity Song of the Week

Matt C: Elephant - Jason Isbell and the 400 Unit

Overall Rating: 10/10

Listen Here

Spotify

Youtube

Overall Review

Jason Isbell is a story teller. His songs conjure emotion and vivid reflections of what it is to be human. Elephant is one woman’s story battling terminal cancer and Jason’s lyrical genius is on full display. The song is sad, funny, sweet and even a little sexy. His guitar playing perfectly matches the underlying melancholy of the story and never fails to bring a lump to my throat and often more than a few tears to my eyes.
