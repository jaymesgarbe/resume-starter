Celebrity Song of the Week

Jack Garbe: DUCKWORTH. - Kendrick Lamar

Overall Rating: 8.8/10

Listen Here

Spotify

Youtube

Overall Review

This song is one of many examples as to why Kendrick’s storytelling is the best of all time. His ability to create a track that has a complex meaning, while also allowing for a casual listen is truly an art. He deliberately places this song at the end of his 2017 album, DAMN, where he walks us through his toughest life lessons. DUCKWORTH wraps up the theme of the album by illustrating a day through Kendrick’s father’s perspective that led to a change in perspective for Kendrick. A beautiful song on an even more beautiful album, I highly recommend.
